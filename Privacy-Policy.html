🔐 Privacy Policy

Calculator Hub Last Updated: June 16, 2026

📋 Introduction

Calculator Hub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our mobile application ("the App") handles your data when you use our Percentage Calculator and Date Calculator features.

We believe in transparency. This policy clearly outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and most importantly — what we do not do with it.

📊 What Data We Collect

Our app collects data only for the purpose of performing calculations. The data we process includes:

  • Percentage Calculator: Numerical values you enter (e.g., percentage value, base number, part/whole values)
  • Date Calculator: Dates you select (start date, end date, and your preference to include/exclude the end day)

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Device information (IMEI, MAC address, serial numbers)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • Usage analytics or crash reports (unless explicitly permitted)

⚙️ How We Use Your Data

The data you enter is used exclusively to:

  • Perform the requested mathematical or date calculations
  • Display the calculated results to you in real-time

We do not:

  • Analyze, mine, or profile your data
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Share your data with any third parties

💾 Where Your Data Is Stored

✅ All data is stored locally on your device. Your data never leaves your phone, tablet, or other device.

The app does not have any servers, cloud storage, or external databases. All calculations are performed entirely on your device using the app's built-in logic.

📱 Your Input ➜ ⚡ Local Calculation ➜ 📲 Your Device Only ➜ 📊 Result Displayed
  • No data is saved — your inputs and results are not stored or cached after you leave the screen
  • No data is transmitted — no network requests are made for calculations
  • No data is backed up — your calculations are not backed up to Google Drive, iCloud, or any cloud service

🚫 Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone.

  • ❌ No third-party analytics services
  • ❌ No advertising networks
  • ❌ No social media platforms
  • ❌ No cloud storage providers
  • ❌ No government or law enforcement agencies (unless required by law)

Since all data stays on your device, there is nothing to share. Your privacy is completely protected.

🔑 App Permissions

Calculator Hub requires no special permissions to function. The app does not request access to:

  • Internet
  • Storage
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Location

This is intentional — we designed the app to be completely self-contained and privacy-first.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect data, there is no risk of inadvertently collecting data from minors.

🛡️ Security

While we don't collect or transmit data, we still take security seriously:

  • No network access — eliminates remote attack vectors
  • No data persistence — no files are written to storage
  • Minimal codebase — less complexity means fewer vulnerabilities
  • Android best practices — we follow Google's security guidelines for app development
